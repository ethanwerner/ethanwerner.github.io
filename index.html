Ochre

Recently, one of the topics that I have held great interest in is ochre. Found across the world, ochres are naturally occurring iron oxide pigments which take on varying yellow and red colors. These pigments, are collected, crushed, and sieved in order to be used in painting. Since I have friends and family who are professional artists, I recently took a hiking trip to the Eastern Hills of Oakland where I was able to collect samples.
